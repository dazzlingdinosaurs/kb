# Resume your Paused CoPilot Membership Plan

```{eval-rst}
.. include:: /apple.rst
```

---

- [Login to the CoPilot app on your iPhone](login-iphone.md)

- An *Account Paused* alert will be displayed, tap **Resume** to access the billing portal

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: png/8d8e620d3f66d3a0d3f9a799d2b09ac4.png
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Resume plan**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: png/ef616af87587e77ee5cadfb89dedc536.png
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Done** to update your account and log in

```{note}
Your CoPilot membership plan is active and payment will occur on the next scheduled billing date
```
