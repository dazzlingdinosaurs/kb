# Edit your Profile Picture

```{eval-rst}
.. include:: /apple.rst
```

---

- On your iPhone, open the CoPilot app

- Go to the **Today** tab, then tap your profile picture in the top left

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: png/987826ee3b1f79def3297f753c753ff7.png
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Edit**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: png/9bee2e975fff29a081460aa13ff7e9c2.png
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Change Profile Photo**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: png/4aca0b479aec6a2074c0ffb704a6da52.png
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Take Photo** to use your camera or **Choose from Gallery** to upload a photo saved on your iPhone
