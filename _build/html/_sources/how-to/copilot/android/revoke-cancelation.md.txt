# Revoke your Cancelation Request

```{eval-rst}
.. include:: /android.rst
```

```{note}
As long as the cancelation has not been executed, you can revoke your cancelation request
```

- On your Android device, open the CoPilot app

- Go to the **Today** tab, then tap the menu button in the top left

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: jpg/6a19822f44ddfcb55d4dfa2fd96e3b80.jpg
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Account settings**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: jpg/cdf56ac7c9e5dbfbe88985a3acdb14c5.jpg
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Billing**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: jpg/390e6a70de455a83dc8b331adba3798a.jpg
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Renew plan** to revoke your cancelation request; your CoPilot membership plan will renew on the next billing date

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: jpg/5d418e63761b787177af45445509069a.jpg
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```
