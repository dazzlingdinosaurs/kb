# Pause your CoPilot Membership Plan

```{eval-rst}
.. include:: /android.rst
```

---

- On your Android device, open the CoPilot app

- Go to the **Today** tab, then tap your profile icon in the top left

<img src="jpg/6a19822f44ddfcb55d4dfa2fd96e3b80.jpg" width="375">

&nbsp;

- Tap **Account settings**

<img src="jpg/cdf56ac7c9e5dbfbe88985a3acdb14c5.jpg" width="375">

&nbsp;

- Tap **Billing**

<img src="jpg/390e6a70de455a83dc8b331adba3798a.jpg" width="375">

&nbsp;

- Tap **Pause**

<img src="jpg/b7dff64b1da6ddddfbf25f5665674949.jpg" width="375">

&nbsp;

- Provide a reason for pausing, select the duration you would like to pause then tap **Pause**

<img src="jpg/04026038e4b461fd3f81206dd6cf2ad0.jpg" width="375">
