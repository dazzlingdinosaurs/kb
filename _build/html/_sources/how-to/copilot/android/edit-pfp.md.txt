# Edit your Profile Picture

```{eval-rst}
.. include:: /android.rst
```

---

- On your Android device, open the CoPilot app

- Go to the **Today** tab, then tap the menu button in the top left

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: jpg/6a19822f44ddfcb55d4dfa2fd96e3b80.jpg
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Account settings**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: jpg/cdf56ac7c9e5dbfbe88985a3acdb14c5.jpg
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Change profile picture**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: jpg/45151ee534a22e69f2493e5051da2c53.jpg
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Camera** to use your camera or **Gallery** to upload a photo saved on your device
