# Delete the CoPilot app on your iPhone

Touch and hold the app

Tap **Remove App**

<img src="jpg/e9f51570762b33011412b8438c9687aeb251316d.jpg" width="350">

Tap **Delete App**, then tap **Delete** to confirm
