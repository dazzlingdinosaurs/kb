# Delete the CoPilot app on your iPhone

1. Touch and hold the app

2. Tap **Remove App**

![iphone-touch-hold-app-edit-menu](jpg/e9f51570762b33011412b8438c9687aeb251316d.jpg)

3. Tap **Delete App**, then tap **Delete** to confirm
