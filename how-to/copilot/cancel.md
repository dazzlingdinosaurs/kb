# Cancel your CoPilot Membership Plan

```{eval-rst}
.. include:: /apple.rst
```

---

- On your iPhone, open the CoPilot app

- Go to the **Today** tab, then tap your profile picture in the top left

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: png/987826ee3b1f79def3297f753c753ff7.png
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Billing**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: png/6453762c34d66bae6e6ca471eb772c82.png
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Tap **Cancel**

```{eval-rst}
.. image:: png/94f8933bf329172896186a63902c55ae.png
   :class: kb-img
   :width: 375px
```

&nbsp;

- Provide a reason for canceling then tap **Submit Cancellation**
